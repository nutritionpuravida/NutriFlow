1-Day Auto Plan
Dropdowns change meal combinations. Exchange totals come from Exchange System. Main meals absorb starch/protein/fat. Snacks use fruit/dairy.
Fruit and dairy servings are assigned with the snack checkboxes and draw from the Exchange System totals. Choose fresh fruit or dried fruit, and milk or yogurt. Crackers replace 1 starch serving only. Oat biscuits and cereal bars replace 1 starch + 1 fat serving. Nuts count as 1 fat exchange and replace 1 tsp olive oil from the main meals — daily calorie total stays unchanged.
