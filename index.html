☀️ SYSTEM — 1-Day Auto Plan
ℹ️ Dropdowns change meal combinations. Exchange totals come from Exchange System. Main meals absorb starch/protein/fat. Snacks use fruit/dairy.
Fruit and dairy servings are assigned with the snack checkboxes and draw from the Exchange System totals. Choose fresh fruit or dried fruit, and milk or yogurt. Starch snacks replace 1 starch serving from the main meals. 🥜 Nuts count as 1 fat exchange and automatically replace 1 tsp olive oil from the main meals — daily calorie total stays unchanged.
